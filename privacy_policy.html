Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Data Collection

At Super Human efficiency, we collect only essential information necessary to provide our cognitive design services. This includes personal identifiers such as name and email address when you voluntarily submit forms, along with technical data like IP addresses and browser information for system optimization.

We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored on secure servers with restricted access, and we never share your personal data with third parties without explicit consent, except as required by law.

Cookies Usage

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure optimal functionality and user experience. These include session cookies for maintaining your preferences and authentication tokens for secure access to personalized features.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain features but will not prevent access to core content. We respect your choices regarding data collection and provide transparent options for managing your privacy preferences.

Analytics & Tracking

We utilize privacy-focused analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our platform. This data helps us optimize the cognitive design experience and improve flow systems. All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated to protect individual privacy.

Our analytics track general usage patterns, page views, and interaction metrics without identifying individual users. This information guides our design intelligence decisions and helps us create more efficient, human-centered digital experiences.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As part of our commitment to digital mindfulness and user empowerment, we fully comply with GDPR regulations. You have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access:Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification:Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure:Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction:Limit how we process your information
  • Right to Data Portability:Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object:Oppose processing of your data for specific purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent:Revoke permission for data processing at any time

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Account information is maintained while your account remains active, and technical logs are typically retained for 90 days for security and optimization purposes.

Upon request for data deletion, we will permanently remove your information from our active systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by legal obligations or legitimate business interests such as dispute resolution.

Security Measures

We implement robust security protocols including SSL encryption, regular security audits, and access controls to protect your data. Our infrastructure follows best practices for cognitive design systems, ensuring both speed and security in data handling.

While we strive for maximum protection, no system is completely secure. We continuously monitor and update our security measures to address emerging threats and maintain the integrity of your personal information.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or prominent website notices at least 30 days before implementation.

Continued use of our services after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information and respect your privacy rights.
